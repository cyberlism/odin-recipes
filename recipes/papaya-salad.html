Papaya Salad

Bursting with Flavor in Every Bite

Papaya Salad, or Som Tum, is a vibrant Thai dish renowned for its combination of spicy, sweet, and tangy flavors.

This salad features crisp green papaya, a medley of fresh vegetables, and a zesty dressing, creating a refreshing harmony of tastes and textures.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium green papaya, peeled and shredded
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup green beans, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2-3 Thai bird chilies, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon palm sugar
  • 2 tablespoons roasted peanuts, crushed
  • 1 tablespoon dried shrimp (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, for garnish

Directions

  1. In a large mortar and pestle, combine garlic and chilies, pounding until they form a coarse paste.
  2. Add palm sugar, fish sauce, and lime juice to the paste, mixing until the sugar dissolves completely.
  3. Incorporate the shredded papaya, green beans, and cherry tomatoes into the mortar, lightly pounding and mixing them with the dressing.
  4. Stir in the dried shrimp, if using, ensuring all ingredients are well-coated with the dressing.
  5. Transfer the salad to a serving dish and sprinkle with crushed peanuts.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves and serve immediately as a refreshing appetizer or side dish.
