Pad Thai

A Stir-Fried Journey of Flavors

Pad Thai is a famous Thai street food dish that features stir-fried rice noodles tossed with a flavorful blend of ingredients.

It's a delightful mix of sweet, sour, and savory flavors, often topped with crushed peanuts for added crunch.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz rice noodles
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 1/2 cup crushed peanuts
  • 1/4 cup green onions, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 lb shrimp or tofu
  • 3 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Soak rice noodles in warm water for 20 minutes, then drain.
  2. In a wok, heat oil and saut√© garlic until golden.
  3. Add shrimp or tofu and cook until done.
  4. Push to the side, crack eggs into the wok, and scramble.
  5. Add noodles, fish sauce, tamarind paste, and brown sugar. Stir-fry until noodles are soft.
  6. Mix in bean sprouts and green onions.
  7. Serve topped with crushed peanuts and lime wedges.
